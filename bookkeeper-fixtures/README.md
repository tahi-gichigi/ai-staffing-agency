# Bookkeeper checker fixture PDFs

18 fixture documents for the Mooch bookkeeper checker demo, generated 25 Apr 2026 from the rows.csv and seeded-errors.md ground truth on the Notion fixture data page (70b53bbdd10c452db7fef3e9ac41c767). Mix of UK SaaS invoices (Adobe, Google, Figma, BT, Zoom, ICO, Adobe Stock, Premier Inn, Amazon) and till/cafe/pub receipts (The Ivy, The Eagle, Costa, Paperchase, Deliveroo, Monmouth Coffee) plus an HMRC payment confirmation letter. Seven of the PDFs (R008, R011, R012, R025, R031, R032, R038) reflect the seeded-errors ground truth rather than the user-entered values in rows.csv, so the agent can flag the deliberate mistakes. Two are deliberately messy to test partial-illegibility handling: R015_paperchase_MESSY.pdf is rotated and partially cropped, and R036_costa_coffee_MESSY.pdf is faded and partially cropped. R042 (Prints of You) is intentionally absent to test the missing-doc case (E8) and no second Adobe Stock PDF is included for R044 to test the duplicate-entry case (E7).
